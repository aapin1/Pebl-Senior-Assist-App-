How Pebl Assistant Works

All-in-one help for tech questions and medicines in three simple steps.

1

Make Pebl Comfortable

Pick a text size, turn audio on or off, and set Pebl up so it matches how you like to read and listen.

2

Get Calm Tech Help

Tap the microphone and ask questions in your own words. Pebl breaks the answer into simple cards you can follow one by one.

3

Keep Medicines In Sight

Add medicines with dosage and times to take them so you always have a clear list to check before taking or bringing to appointments.
