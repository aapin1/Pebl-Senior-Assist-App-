Built Specifically for Seniors

Every feature designed with accessibility and ease-of-use in mind

Adjustable Large Text

Choose from multiple text sizes for comfortable reading. Perfect for those with vision difficulties - text is clear, bold, and easy to see.

Voice-First Design

No typing needed! Simply speak your question naturally. Our advanced speech recognition understands how you talk, not just keywords.

Audio Read-Aloud

Every answer is read aloud to you with adjustable speed and volume. Perfect for seniors who prefer listening over reading.

Interactive Step Cards

Instructions broken into simple, digestible steps with progress tracking. Move at your own pace - no rushing, no confusion.

Extra-Large Buttons

All buttons are oversized and easy to tap - designed for seniors with arthritis or limited dexterity. No more missing tiny buttons!

Patient & Encouraging

Never feel embarrassed about asking questions. Pebl celebrates your progress and explains things as many times as you need.

100% Free to Use

Completely free with only minimal, family-friendly ads every few questions. No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no credit card required.

iPhone & iPad Expert

Specialized for Apple devices with detailed instructions for iOS features. We know exactly where every button is on your device.

Your Privacy Protected

Your conversations stay private and secure. We never store personal information or share your data with anyone.

How Pebl Works

Getting help is as easy as 1-2-3

1

Tap & Speak

Open the app and tap the microphone button. Ask your question in your own words - "How do I make a phone call?" or "Why isn't my WiFi working?"

2

Get Clear Instructions

Pebl analyzes your question and provides step-by-step instructions tailored to your specific device and situation.

3

Follow Along

Work through each step at your own pace. Ask follow-up questions anytime if you need more clarification.
